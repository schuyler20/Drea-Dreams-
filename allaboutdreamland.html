DreamLand

Drea Dreams is from Dreamland and she’s the daughter of the king and queen Michael and Anna dreams. In this world everything is built off of your imagination so the bigger the imagination the better. So people are divided into social hierarchy based off of how imaginative their brains are. Drea is extremely gifted because her mind expands to many places and she can do many things with her mind so that’s why she is very popular in Dreamland.
